AI-powered University Advisory System

Using Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLM) for Personalized Academic and Career Guidance

This project focuses on the development of an AI-powered academic advisor, designed to provide personalized and context-aware academic and career guidance. By integrating LLM fine-tuning, knowledge distillation, and hybrid embedding-based retrieval, this system dynamically interprets student degree works, course plans, and institutional policies to deliver highly tailored recommendations.

Additionally, this research explores cutting-edge technologies, including multi-modal learning, scalable inference, prompt engineering optimization, and domain-specific adaptation. These innovations aim to push the boundaries of LLMs in real-world applications.

Key focus areas include improving model efficiency, reducing hallucinations, and enhancing knowledge-grounded reasoning, making this advisory system a robust tool for academic and career decision-making.
