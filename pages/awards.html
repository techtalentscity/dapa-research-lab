Awards and Recognitions

Celebrating excellence and innovation in research and technology.

First Place Innovation Expo Award

The lab won first place at Morgan TechFest 2024 Innovation Expo with its groundbreaking research, 'Track-Based Autonomous Wheelchair Navigation for Airport Environments.' This AI-driven system integrates advanced technologies such as Ultralytics YOLOv8, QR Code navigation, LiDAR, and cameras to enhance mobility in crowded spaces like airports, ensuring efficient path-following and obstacle avoidance. The research's exceptional innovation earned a $2,000 cash prize.
