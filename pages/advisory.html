Research Overview

Using Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLM) for Personalized Academic and Career Guidance

AI-Powered Guidance

This project focuses on developing an AI-powered academic advisor, designed to provide personalized and context-aware academic and career guidance. Using advanced LLM fine-tuning and knowledge distillation techniques, we explore cutting-edge technologies including multi-modal learning, scalable inference, and prompt engineering optimization. Our goal is to enhance knowledge-grounded reasoning and create a robust tool for academic and career decision-making.
