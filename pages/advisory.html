AI-powered University Advisory System

Advancing Academic Guidance through Artificial Intelligence

Research Overview

Using Generative AI and Large Language Models (LLM) for Personalized Academic and Career Guidance

AI-Powered Guidance

This project focuses on developing an AI-powered academic advisor, designed to provide personalized and context-aware academic and career guidance. Using advanced LLM fine-tuning and knowledge distillation techniques.

Advanced Technologies

Exploring cutting-edge technologies including multi-modal learning, scalable inference, prompt engineering optimization, and domain-specific adaptation to push the boundaries of LLMs in real-world applications.

Key Improvements

Focusing on improving model efficiency, reducing hallucinations, and enhancing knowledge-grounded reasoning to create a robust tool for academic and career decision-making.
