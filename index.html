Lab Overview

At DEPA Research Lab, we focus on gaining insights and automating complex systems through applied research in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and other cutting-edge AI techniques.

  • Developing flexible data-gathering and fusion techniques from heterogeneous sources.
  • Creating models and algorithms for general and embedded systems.
  • Building dynamic visualization engines for advanced user interaction.

Core Research Areas

IoT Data Analytics

Analyze and visualize complex IoT networks for actionable insights.

Cybersecurity

Secure networks through advanced analytics and visualization techniques.

Machine Learning

Develop models for decision support, simulation, and predictive analytics.

Computer Vision

Enhance decision-making with advanced visual analytics and object detection.

Interactive Visualization

Use virtual, mixed, and augmented reality to visualize data effectively.

Computer Vision Applications

Pose Estimation

Track and identify objects in challenging environments using multi-layer vision algorithms.

Traffic Analytics

Automate vehicle and pedestrian tracking using real-time video analysis.

Scene Perception
